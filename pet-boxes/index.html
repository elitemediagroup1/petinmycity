Why pet owners love subscription boxes

Monthly pet subscription boxes deliver curated treats, toys, chews, and accessories tailored to your pet’s size, age, and play style. They’re a fun way to discover new products, keep your pet engaged, and avoid trips to the store.

What is usually included

🍪 Treats

Healthy treats, jerky, dental sticks, and seasonal goodies.

🧸 Toys

Plush, rope, squeaky, or interactive toys sized for your pet.

🦴 Chews

Long-lasting bully sticks, antlers, or rubber chews.

🎄 Themes

Holiday, seasonal, or special-occasion themed boxes.
