Pet Insurance Questions from Seattle Pet Owners

How much does pet insurance cost in Seattle?

Pet insurance in Seattle typically costs $45–$65 per month for dogs and $20–$40 per month for cats. Seattle is one of the higher-cost pet insurance markets because veterinary costs in the city are above the national average. Use PetInsurer.com to compare quotes for your specific Seattle pet.

Did Trupanion start in Seattle?

Yes — Trupanion was founded in Seattle and remains headquartered here. They are one of the most established pet insurance companies in North America with a particular strength in the Pacific Northwest market. Their direct vet payment model — where they pay your vet directly instead of reimbursing you — was pioneered in Seattle.

Why does Seattle spend so much on pets?

Seattle consistently ranks among the top cities for pet spending in the US. The combination of high household incomes from the tech industry, a deeply ingrained outdoor and dog-friendly culture, and Seattle's exceptionally pet-friendly infrastructure — off-leash parks, pet-friendly transit, and welcoming retail — creates a market where pet spending is normalized at a higher level than most cities.

What are the best dog parks in Seattle?

Magnuson Park off-leash area is the largest dog park in Seattle with over 9 acres of off-leash terrain including a waterfront swimming area. Marymoor Park in Redmond has one of the largest off-leash areas in Washington State at 40 acres. Regrade Park in Belltown and Cal Anderson Park on Capitol Hill are popular urban off-leash spots.
