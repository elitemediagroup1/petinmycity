Why we built this

Pet owners deserve a free honest resource

PetsInMyCity was built by the team at Elite Media Group LLC — the same company behind ConsumerSupportHelp.com. Pet owners searching for local resources were being sent to generic national directories with no local knowledge. So we built something better.

Pet insurance comparison, vet finding, adoption resources, and product recommendations — all free, all local, no spam ever.
