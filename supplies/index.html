First-week pet supply checklist

\n
\n

🐶 New puppy

  • Puppy food (consult vet on brand)
  • Food and water bowls
  • Properly sized crate
  • Collar, ID tag, leash (4–6 ft)
  • Puppy pads or potty trays
  • Chew toys (variety of textures)
  • Soft bed and blanket
  • Enzyme cleaner for accidents
\n

🐈 New kitten

  • Kitten food
  • Food and water bowls
  • Litter box plus litter
  • Scratching post
  • Carrier for vet trips
  • Toys (wand, balls, mice)
  • Bed or covered hideout
  • Cat tree or perch
\n

🐕 Adult dog

  • Age-appropriate food
  • Bowls and feeding mat
  • Crate or bed
  • Collar, harness, leash
  • Long-lasting chews
  • Brushes and nail clippers
  • Flea/tick/heartworm prevention
  • Poop bags
\n

🐱 Adult cat

  • Adult food (wet and dry options)
  • Multiple litter boxes (1 per cat + 1)
  • Scratching posts (multiple locations)
  • Water fountain (encourages hydration)
  • Carrier
  • Interactive toys
  • Grooming brush
  • Cat-safe houseplants only
