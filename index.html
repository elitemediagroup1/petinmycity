Common Questions

Is petsinmycity.com free to use?

Yes — completely free for pet owners. We connect you with pet insurance providers, local vets, adoption resources, and pet services at no cost. We earn referral fees from our partners when you choose to use their services.

How do I find pet services in my city?

Enter your city or ZIP code in the search bar above and select the service you need. We'll show you local options for insurance, vets, adoption, grooming, boarding, and training near you.

What is the best pet insurance for my dog?

The best pet insurance depends on your pet's age, breed, and your budget. We recommend using PetInsurer.com to compare plans from multiple providers side by side — it takes about 60 seconds and shows you the best options for your specific pet.

How do I find a vet near me?

Use the Vet Finder section on your city page. Enter your city or ZIP code, select Find a Vet, and we'll connect you with licensed local veterinary practices in your area.

Is petsinmycity.com affiliated with any specific pet insurance company?

We partner with multiple pet insurance providers including PetInsurer, Trupanion, Fetch, and others. We present all options fairly so you can choose the plan that best fits your pet and budget. Our goal is to help you find the right coverage, not to push one specific provider.
