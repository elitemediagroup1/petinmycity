Common questions

Questions pet owners ask us

Is petsinmycity.com free to use?

Yes — completely free for pet owners. We connect you with pet insurance providers, local vets, adoption resources, and pet services at no cost. We earn referral fees from our partners when you choose to use their services.

What is the best pet insurance right now?

It depends on your pet's age, breed, and your budget. The fastest way to find out is to use PetInsurer.com to compare plans from multiple providers at once — it takes about 60 seconds and shows you the best options for your specific pet. Trupanion and Fetch are both excellent for comprehensive coverage.

When should I get pet insurance for my new pet?

As soon as possible — ideally within 14 days of adoption or purchase. Most policies have waiting periods of 14–30 days before coverage begins, and conditions diagnosed before enrollment are excluded as pre-existing. The younger and healthier your pet is at enrollment, the lower your premiums will be.

How do I find a vet near me?

Enter your ZIP code in the search bar at the top of the page. We'll connect you with licensed local veterinary practices that are accepting new patients in your area.

Does petsinmycity.com sell my information?

No. Your information is never sold or shared with third parties. We connect you with one local provider based on your needs — not a marketplace that auctions your details to dozens of companies. Elite Media Group LLC respects your privacy.
