Quick answers

How much does it cost to adopt?

$50–$500 typically — includes spay/neuter, first vaccines, and microchip. Far cheaper than buying from a breeder.

What if I've never owned a pet before?

Shelters love first-time owners. Most have counselors who help match you with a pet that fits your lifestyle and experience level.

Can I adopt online?

Yes — Petfinder and Adopt-a-Pet let you browse, message shelters, and start applications online. Final approval usually requires an in-person visit.
